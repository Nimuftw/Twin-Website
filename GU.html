February 2nd, 1999

Next Previous

We were born on February 2nd, 1999 at Massachusetts General Hospital to Pratik and Trupti Multani. At just six months old, our family moved to San Diego, California on account of new job opportunities. Since then, we have flowered as identical, mirror image twins, with very similar aspirations in engineering and biomedical science.

We attended Del Mar Pines for elementary school and then moved to the Grauer School for middle school. We were able to flourish during our middle school years as the Grauer school provided an accepting and intimate atmosphere with only 150 students from 6th-12th grade. Close relationships with our teachers and classmates allowed us to explore our inner selves and outgrow our reserved nature.

We are currently attending Torrey Pines High School. The transition from a small private school to a public school with over 2,000 kids was challenging, no doubt. The experience, however, has taught us that education can be as rigorous or as simple as we want it to be. It is through hard work, independence, and intrinsic motivation that the two of us have been able to explore and expand our intellectual limits.
